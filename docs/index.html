A comfortable home for Mesa Verde visitors!

Our great condo is located in Cortez Colorado , overlooking the Hawkins Preserve with direct views to Mesa Verde and close access to all of the sights in the Four Corners area.

The condo has open living space with abundant light and great sunsets. There is a private patio for evening relaxation, and hiking paths from condo's door.

We are in the Cortez / Mesa Verde / Four Corners Region of Southwest Colorado, USA. The Cortez Airport is five minutes away, Durango 50 miles, Telluride Ski Area 70 miles.

We cater to long-term visitors, who are working or vacationing, and looking to stay for a week, a month, or more. Contact us for more information on rates and availability.
